Mirko Filipović "Cro Cop"

One of the greatest Heavyweight Kickboxers and MMA fighters

Summary of Cro Cop's carrer:

  • 1996 : At 22 years old, started his kickboxing career in K-1
  • 2001 : Began his switch to MMA in Pride
  • 2001 : Kept fighting for K-1, Pride and Inoki Bom-Ba-Ye
  • 2006 : Became the Pride Open-Weight Grand Prix Champion
  • 2007 : Started to fight for the UFC
  • 2008 : Started to fight for the Dream (successor of Pride)
  • 2011 : Retired from UFC and MMA
  • 2012 : Became the K-1 World Grand Prix Champion
  • 2015 : Came back to UFC and got suspended after the first fight due to admitting doping.
  • 2016 : Started to fight for Rizin and became the Rizin Openweight Grand Prix Champion
  • 2019 : At 44 years old, had a stroke after fighting Roy Nelson and announced his final retirement

Mirko keeps a healthy life and has been training his son.
